Resumator

Resumator is a web app that makes creating resumes super easy. Working with a team, I handled the front-end development using React, JavaScript, and HTML. The app lets users fill out forms with their info, like work experience, education, and skills, and then generates a nicely formatted PDF resume they can download right away. I built the interactive forms with real-time validation. The website is a one-stop solution for anyone who needs to create a professional resume quickly.

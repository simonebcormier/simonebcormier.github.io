Raycasting in Maya

Casting a ray from the arrows pivot along single surface normal and drawing a NURBS curve object to visualize the ray. When the ray intersects different objects in the scene, it prints the object name it intersected and area of the intersected triangle. All mathematical equations to calculate area are coded using built in math functions. All intersections are limited to the objects in front of the raycaster, frontfaces and bounded polygon edges.



Simone Cormier - Programmer and Designer

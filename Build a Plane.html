Build-A-Plane App

An app created on Figma with a team. I was the lead designer and created the prototype on Figma. The idea was the create an application that can be downloaded at the Canadian Aviation and Space Museum in Ottawa to give the visitors and interactive experience. Users can use AR to scan plane parts and collect different planes
